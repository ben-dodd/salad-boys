Equipment Guide NEW!

Ever wondered what gear we use? Here's a breakdown of our equipment!

Joe Sampson - Guitar

Fender Jazzmaster (1965 Reissue)

Main guitar for most recordings and live shows. Stock pickups with a modified rhythm circuit.

Fender Twin Reverb

Clean tones for days. Usually runs at around 4-5 for live shows.

Effects Pedals

  • Boss TU-2 Tuner
  • Ibanez Tube Screamer TS-9
  • Electro-Harmonix Big Muff
  • Boss DD-3 Digital Delay
  • MXR Phase 90

Ben Woods - Bass

Fender Precision Bass (1978)

Workhorse bass with flatwound strings for that warm, punchy tone.

Ampeg SVT Classic + 8x10 Cabinet

Nothing beats the classic Ampeg sound for rock bass.

James Sullivan - Drums

Ludwig Classic Maple Kit

  • 22" x 16" Bass Drum
  • 12" x 8" Rack Tom
  • 14" x 14" Floor Tom
  • 14" x 6.5" Supraphonic Snare

Zildjian Cymbals

  • 14" New Beat Hi-Hats
  • 20" A Custom Crash
  • 22" K Custom Ride
