Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is the new album coming out?

A: We're currently in the studio working on new material. Stay tuned to our News page for updates!

Q: How can I book Salad Boys for a show?

A: For booking inquiries, please email us at booking@saladboys.com. Please include the date, venue, and any other relevant details.

Q: Where can I buy your albums?

A: Our albums are available at most local record stores in New Zealand. You can also find them online through Bandcamp and various streaming services.

Q: Do you have any merchandise?

A: Yes! T-shirts and other merch are available at our live shows. We're working on setting up an online store soon!

Q: Can I use your music in my video/project?

A: Please contact us at saladboys@gmail.com with details about your project and we'll get back to you.

Q: How did the band get its name?

A: That's a long story involving a late night, too many drinks, and a very memorable salad. We'll tell you all about it at a show sometime!

Q: What are your influences?

A: We draw from a wide range of artists including Guided By Voices, Pavement, The Clean, The Bats, and lots of classic New Zealand indie rock.

Have a question that's not answered here? Email us at saladboys@gmail.com!
